Notes on: Toward Renewable Assessments (David Wiley, 2016)

Interesting concept of "disposable assessments": papers or other assessments that are thrown away once given a grade. I had not encountered this term before.

The opposite, "renewable assessment" would then be something which creates value or gets released into the world in some form that other people might find useful.

  • Tells people: your work matters; disposable assessments tell us that it doesn't, you are never given a taste of making a small difference on the world or your local community
